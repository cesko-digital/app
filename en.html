About Česko.Digital

We are a community of top developers, designers and production people who want to use their free time to help state and non-governmental organizations and make the Czech Republic a better place to live. We have behind us worldwide success and a combined total of hundreds of years’ experience. And we know that with the help of IT we can achieve great things, which cost less but work better.
